Photon

2019-24

Photon fixed on wall with case open to show it's circuits
Photon on lab floor during it's demo video

Photon is a Smart Home switch. Since it controls light bulbs, it is named after the particle of light. It is my first IOT device.

Following are it's features:

  • It is connected to wifi and mqtt broker in our lan. So, can be controlled from anywhere inside home.
  • Supports 2 channels, which means it can control upto 2 light bulbs
  • Supports pir sensor. So, motion can be used to trigger the lights.
  • Fully integrated with SHARP
  • SHARP integration makes automations seemless. Just set the time and never worry about turning the lights on again.
  • It has several leds to show status of lights and the network connection.
  • It also has a buzzer to provide audio feedback when the device is turned on. The buzzer can be used as an alarm as well.
  • Supports Over the air updates for the firmware.
